Healthy Reminders

This application will remind you to take healthy breaks.

60 min
Standing up and moving around helps to improve circulation and prevent stiffness. Try to stand up and walk around for a few minutes.
120 min
Stretching helps to relieve muscle tension and improve flexibility. Focus on stretching your back, neck, and legs.
20 min
The 20-20-20 rule helps to reduce eye strain. Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds.
90 min
Staying hydrated is important for overall health. Aim to drink a glass of water every 90 minutes.
240 min
An ergonomic setup helps to prevent strain and injury. Ensure your chair, desk, and monitor are set up correctly.
